About Ashland

Ashland is a friendly city located in southern Kansas, known for its beautiful landscapes, welcoming residents, and rich history. Whether you're visiting for the scenic views, local events, or just passing through, Ashland offers a peaceful retreat and a taste of true Midwestern hospitality.

Local Attractions

  • Big Basin Prairie Preserve
  • Clark State Fishing Lake
  • Historic Main Street
  • Annual Ashland Rodeo
